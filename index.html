Grand Gerbil

  • Wise

  • Master in Gerbil-Fu (The art of soothing somebody)

Grand Gerbil alerts the crew when somebody needs help, or somebody is being mean.

Bunny Buddies

  • Augustus is the leader of the pack

  • Never break apart from each other

The Bunny Buddies help people in need around the town, and plays with the town kids in their free time.

Jack Moriarty

  • Parental figure for everyone else (Makes sure no one gets hurt, feeds them, etc)

  • Loves cats

Jack cares for other animals that are hurt or struggling. He also works around the forest, like the bakery.

The Ooga Booga

  • Takes mushrooms from unsuspecting victims

  • Uses it to make more Ooga Boogas

  • How to beat: Teaching it the way of sharing instead of taking, and helping it learn what it did wasn't right.

The Ooga Booga was created when somebody stole it's mushrooms. The Ooga Booga decided that if it can't have mushrooms, nobody can.

Rosemary

  • Sweet

  • Shy

  • Affectionate

Provides sweet lavender milk for everybody to share in the neighborhood.

Bo

  • Easily scared

  • Loves Sharing

Was found in the frightening forest by Jack Moriarty, and immediately warmed up to him.
